About Me

I am currently a Supply Chain Info. and Analytics student in the Krannert School of Management at Purdue University. I created this website to share the various projects I've been working on related to Business Analytics and Data Science. In my spare time, I enjoy videomaking, cutting hair, and watching the Boston Celtics.

Exploring US Bikeshare Data

A program that generates statistics based on 2017 bikeshare data across Chicago, New York City, and Washington, D.C. Written in Python using Pandas and NumPy.

YouTube Data Analysis

Tableau charts, dashboards, and stories analyzing 400,000 data entries about trending YouTube videos in each US State.

New York Stock Exchange Data Analysis

Excel Workbook containing sheets that include Income Statement and Forecast Model for Ebay to forecast upcoming revenue, gross profit, and operating income based on historical financial data. Additionally, insights were generated from the visualizations created using summary statistics based on 25,000+ data entries about historical, financial data from S&P 500 companies.
